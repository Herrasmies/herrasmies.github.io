Peer-reviewed publications

I’ve (co)authored 73 scientific papers, 63 of which have been peer-reviewed (21 as first author, 15 as second author, and 9 as last author). My papers focus on 1) gambling, games, and behavioral addictions, 2) social psychology and moral psychology of robotics, 3) emotions, decision-making, and psychophysiological correlates of cognition, and 4) psychology of deception.

My papers are listed below topic-wise, though some papers fit multiple topics. If you want a paper you can’t access, I’ll be happy to email you a copy.
